We recruit during the Fall and Spring quarters. All majors/backgrounds are welcome to apply.

Fall 2019 Recruitment Calendar

Case Workshop

Thursday 4/4 | 4:30 PM

We will be partnering with Career Peers to host a case workshop open to the public where we will cover the mechanics of a case interview and strategies for success. We strongly advise you attend if you have no casing experience.

Career Center Room 200 | Smart Casual

Info Session

Thursday 4/4 | 4:30 PM

Our info session will touch upon what UConsulting does and what it can offer to its members. We will also touch upon what we are looking for in an applicant with time allotted for networking.

James West Alumni Center | Business Casual

Application Due

Thursday 4/4 | 4:30 PM

Our application consists of a resume and cover letter (optional). Please include your GPA on your resume. We recommend that you use your cover letter to expand upon anything you feel is missing from your resume. (E.g. Don't have much work experience? What having you been doing with your time instead?)
