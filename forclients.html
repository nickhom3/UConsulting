Project Workflow

Each client is assigned a project team of 4-5 trained consultants led by a Project Manager for the duration of 8 - 10 weeks. A typical project will follow the steps of:

(1) problem definition, (2) framework creation, (3) data and knowledge gathering, (4) data analysis, (5) strategy recommendation creation, and (6) client presentation.
